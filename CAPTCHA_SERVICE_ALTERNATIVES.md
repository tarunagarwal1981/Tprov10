# CAPTCHA Service Alternatives to Google reCAPTCHA

## Why Consider Alternatives?

**Google reCAPTCHA Issues:**
- ❌ **Free tier reduced** - Only 10,000 assessments/month (was 1 million)
- ❌ **Paid after free tier** - $8/month for 100K assessments
- ❌ **Privacy concerns** - Tracks users across sites
- ❌ **Complex setup** - Key type mismatches, domain verification
- ❌ **User friction** - "I'm not a robot" checkbox can be annoying
- ❌ **Reliability issues** - Sometimes fails to load, timeout errors

**Benefits of Alternatives:**
- ✅ **Better free tiers** - More generous limits
- ✅ **Privacy-focused** - GDPR compliant, no tracking
- ✅ **Easier setup** - Simpler integration
- ✅ **Better UX** - Less user friction
- ✅ **More reliable** - Better uptime
- ✅ **Cost-effective** - Better pricing
